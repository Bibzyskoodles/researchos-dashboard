# The ResearchOS Manifesto

ResearchOS exists to make research organizations dramatically more capable.

We do not celebrate software. We celebrate research.
We do not celebrate AI. We celebrate better decisions.

We speak the language of researchers, never the language of infrastructure.
We price completed work, not compute.
We measure impact, not usage.
We adapt to organizations rather than forcing organizations to adapt to us.

Ada exists to make researchers better — not to replace them.

The platform is adaptive by nature. A field supervisor in Lagos, a research
director at a global NGO, and a consumer-insights lead at an FMCG company should
each open ResearchOS and feel it was built for them — same engine underneath,
different experience on top.

Every feature must answer one question:

> **Does this help someone produce better research?**

If not, it doesn't belong.
