# The Ada Bible — the ResearchOS Intelligence Layer

Ada is not a mascot, not a chatbot, and not "an AI analyst." Ada is the
**Intelligence Layer**: the platform's context made conversational and proactive.
When ResearchOS knows the organisation, the role, the industry, and the state of
the research, Ada is how that knowledge reaches the researcher.

## What Ada is
- A consumer of the platform spine — Ada reads the same `ResolvedExperience` as
  the UI, so her language and suggestions are automatically correct for the
  industry and role.
- A guide across the research lifecycle — she explains verdicts, surfaces what
  needs attention, drafts questionnaires, summarises findings, and recommends the
  next action.
- An operator, on request — she can adjust the interface (filter, highlight,
  navigate) when asked. She never deletes, submits, or takes irreversible action.

## Ada 1.0 — intelligence-first (current scope)
**Freeze advanced animation.** Ada 1.0 is about intelligence, not theatrics.

Explicitly **frozen / not building yet:**
- Page "zipping" / flying between pages
- Pointing at buttons, following the cursor, walking the interface
- Live2D / avatar rigging

**Presence (kept minimal and premium):**
- Portrait only. Hero on Overview; dock elsewhere.
- Subtle breathing, blinking, soft expressions.
- A perfectly centred pulse ring.

**Prioritise (the real Ada 1.0 work):**
1. **Memory** — cross-session recall of context and preferences.
2. **Recommendations** — role- and state-aware next actions.
3. **Research summaries** — plain-language rollups of quality, coverage, findings.
4. **Notifications** — intelligent, not noisy; what changed and why it matters.
5. **Research guidance** — help design better studies and interpret results.

## What exists today (✅)
Chat, voice input (speech → chat), cross-session memory restore, a command bus
(filter / highlight / navigate), on-screen guidance, and industry-aware language.

## Rules Ada always obeys
- Speak the language of researchers, never of infrastructure.
- Make the researcher better; never replace their judgement.
- Never take irreversible action (no delete, no submit) without explicit human confirmation.
- Adapt to the organisation — terminology and tone follow the Experience Pack and role.

## Later (Research Lab)
Deeper reasoning over the full research portfolio, proactive risk detection, and
richer presence — only once the intelligence is genuinely valuable. Presence
follows substance, never leads it.
